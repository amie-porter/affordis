About Us

Affordis Ltd is a dedicated property management company focused on delivering a streamlined, dependable service to both landlords and tenants. We understand that effective property management requires clear communication, prompt action, and accountability. Our approach ensures that properties are maintained to a high standard while landlords receive consistent income and transparent reporting.

Our Core Services

Full Property Management

We offer a comprehensive service covering all day-to-day management responsibilities. From tenant communication to maintenance resolution, we ensure your property is professionally managed at all times.

Rent Collection & Financial Management

We manage the complete rent collection process, ensuring timely collection of rental payments, regular landlord disbursements, and clear financial statements. Our systems ensure consistent cash flow and full transparency.

Maintenance & Contractor Coordination

We liaise with a network of trusted contractors to respond promptly to maintenance issues, obtain competitive quotations, and oversee works to completion. This ensures properties are maintained efficiently and cost-effectively.

Tenant Management

We act as the primary point of contact for tenants, handling day-to-day enquiries, maintenance reporting, and communication throughout the tenancy. This helps maintain positive tenant relationships and long-term occupancy.

Landlord Support & Reporting

We keep landlords fully informed with regular updates, financial summaries, and maintenance reports. Our goal is to provide clarity, control, and confidence at all times.
